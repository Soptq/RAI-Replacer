Regex Replacement Commands

How to use rules?

Basically you need to input a regex that helps the extension to locate USD, and a replacement that helps the extension understand how to replace. The inputted regex is just like regular regex, you can test your regex at Regex101. On the other hand, the replacement is a string with format specifiers %p and %s, where %p will be the converted price and %s will be smart whitespace after formatting. Notice that the regex doesn't need any capturing group, the only missing of it is to extract the whole string with/without number.

Commands: ✎ stands for rule editing, 🗑 stands for rule deleting, ↑ and ↓ stand for rule priority setting (executing from top to down).
